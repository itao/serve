About SERVE Brief history, how we roll, and rules.

History

SERVE is a volleyball club started by a group of students who, quite simply, just wanted to play some volleyball! What started off as random pick-up games quickly grew into one of the university's biggest club!

How it work

Games are divided as competitive and recreational, and rotate based on "King's Court". No obligated attendance, just come whenever you feel like playing some volleyball!

Rules

# Commandment
1 The first rule of SERVE Club is, you do not talk about SERVE Club.
2 The second rule of SERVE Club is, you DO NOT talk about SERVE Club.
3 If someone says stop, goes limp, taps out, the rally is over.
4 ALL serves are in.
